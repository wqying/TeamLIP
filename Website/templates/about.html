About Dabbling in Finance

an IrvineHacks project by UCI students

Interested in learning more about financial literacy?

Financial literacy is important for everyone! With Dabbling in Finance, we're here to help you understand it better.

It's beneficial to be knowledgeable about the pillars of financial literacy: saving, investing, and debt.

You may be unfamiliar with these concepts, but we all have to start somewhere!
