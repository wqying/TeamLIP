Loans and Debt

Learn about borrowing loans and reducing debt

Are all debts bad? No! But... there are risks. Take these steps to manage debt effectively.

Be mindful of all accounts. List out all your debts and their interest rates.

Take a credit report. There are plenty of sites that offer free quotes. It's better to have a

credit score at least between 690 and 720, allowing for lower interest rates and easier access to loans.

Referring back to budgeting, keep track of your expenses and spending habits.

Look for consolidation opportunities. Consolidate multiple high-interest loans into one loan with a lower interest rate.

Strategize your approach! Would you pay off balances with high or low interest rates first?

Make choices that will benefit you the most based on your current financial situation.
