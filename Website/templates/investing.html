INVESTING?

Why should I invest?

While it may seem intimidating, investing is an opportunity to start building your wealth over time,

gain financial freedom, and take a risk that may result in high returns!

How can I start investing?

First, think about your short-term and long-term goals and how much money you're willing to invest.

Familiarize yourself by doing research! Look into a company's earnings, profits, and projected growth.

After doing research, consider opening an investing account with a broker.

What should I invest in?

Diversify your portfolio! It's important to reduces risk and increases the chances of a higher return.

As a beginner, some good options are low-cost stock mutual funds, index funds, and exchange-traded funds (EFTs).

Investing isn't just limited to stocks. It's also important to consider options like a high-yield savings account

or especially invest in your future by contributing to your 401(k) or other retirement plans!
